Penny can give financial tips. These include:
- exceeding budget or staying under budget
- notable patterns in spending
- possible unnecessary spends
- how the user compares to other users of similar age group
- what the highest/lowest spent category is
- subscription services the user pays for
- most often visited place eg a shop or restaurant
- allows for a calendar to track each days' activities to explain possible over budget spending

Penny lines:

Introductory:
"Hi! I'm Penny. I'm your go-to finance helper for [APPNAME]."
"Would you like a quick tutorial on how [APPNAME] works?"

Exceeding budget:
"This month, you've exceeded your budget of [BUDGET] by [EXCEEDAMOUNT]. :(."
